It is hard to find a person who has not heard the words: mobile phone, communicator, smartphone, mobile phone. At the same time, for many people there is confusion in defining the difference of these terms. The most popular designation of a device for making calls is phone, in turn, if the phone is not stationary, it is called mobile, because of ÑŽ autonomous work and the possibility of moving or cellular, which emphasises its work in the system of cellular communication. But, technology has developed rapidly and in a few years the term "operating system" appeared.
